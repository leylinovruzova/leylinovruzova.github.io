Hour Of Code

Data Avengers

On November 7 at 10:30 a.m., in Garadagh District No. 253 secondary school, as Group 21, we held our #Hour of Code training dedicated to the December 4-10 computer science week. The members of our group were Jalal Asgarzade, Vugar Mirzayev, Farid Aliyev and Leyli Novruzova. 20 8th grade students actively participated in our event. First, we started our training by providing information about ADA University. Then, with the help of the Code.org website, we showed the children how interesting and convenient programming is. Our training was fun as well as informative. We would like to express our deep gratitude to the entire school staff who made it possible for us to pass this training!
